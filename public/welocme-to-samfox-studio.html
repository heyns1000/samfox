Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my work?

Navigate to the Submissions page, select your competition category, upload your files, fill in the required information, and click Submit. You'll receive a confirmation email once your submission is processed.

Can I submit to multiple competitions?

Yes! Your subscription tier determines how many competitions you can enter. Starter members can submit to 2 competitions, while Professional and Enterprise members have unlimited access.

What file formats are accepted?

We accept PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, PSD, SVG, and other common design file formats. Maximum file size is 50MB per submission. For video submissions, MP4 and MOV formats are supported.

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected by our panel of industry experts based on creativity (40%), technical excellence (30%), concept clarity (20%), and impact (10%). The judging process is blind to ensure fairness.

What rights do I retain over my work?

You retain full ownership and copyright of your submissions. By entering, you grant us permission to display your work in our gallery and promotional materials with proper attribution.
